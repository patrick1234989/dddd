About Us

Who We Are

CPEC SAINT BABETH is a private educational institution located in Kigali, Rwanda. The school provides Ordinary Level education and technical training in Software Development and Multimedia.


Our mission is to provide quality education, practical skills and modern technology knowledge that prepares students for the future.
